User Journey

  • User enters the site
  • Opens the dress-up editor
  • Applies clothing items to the avatar how ever they wish
  • Saves outfit
  • Publishes outfit to the gallery
  • Enters the voting room
  • Votes update in real time
  • Rankings show top outfits
    • This flow guided the mockups and the structure of the HTML/CSS.

Target Audience

Kids and style-loving adults who enjoy playful dress-up experiences.
